Scorpio:
Unrelenting, strange and ful of contrasts, often egoistic,
aggressive, noble, broad horizon, unexpected reactions,
spontancous, unlimited ambition, no flexibility,
difficult and uncommon partner, not always liked but
often admired, ingenious, strategist, very jealous and
passionate, no compromise.

Strengths:
resourceful, brave, passionate, stubborn, a true friend.

Weakness:
distrusting, jealous, secretive, violent.

Likes:
truth, facts, being right, long time friends,
teasing, a grand passion.

Dislikes:
dishonesty, revealing secrets, passive people.
